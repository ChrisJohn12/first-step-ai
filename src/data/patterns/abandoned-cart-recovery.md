# Abandoned Cart Recovery Automation

## Trigger Conditions
- Customer adds item(s) to cart but does not complete checkout
- Cart has been inactive for 1+ hours
- Store processes ~200 orders/month with no existing cart follow-up sequence
- Cart abandonment rate above 60% (Shopify average is ~70%)

## Recommendation
Use **Klaviyo** to set up a 3-step abandoned cart flow:
- **Email 1** — 1 hour after abandonment: friendly reminder with cart contents and direct checkout link
- **Email 2** — 24 hours after abandonment: highlight product benefits or social proof (reviews)
- **Email 3** — 72 hours after abandonment: limited-time incentive (e.g., 10% off or free shipping)

## Expected Time Savings
6–10 hours per week (eliminates manual outreach, recovers revenue passively, and requires no per-order intervention once live)

## Setup Steps
1. Install Klaviyo from the Shopify App Store and connect it to your store
2. In Klaviyo, navigate to **Flows** and select the pre-built "Abandoned Cart" flow template
3. Customize the three email steps — add product images, cart summary blocks, and your brand voice
4. Set time delays: Email 1 at 1 hour, Email 2 at 24 hours, Email 3 at 72 hours
5. Add a discount code to Email 3 using Shopify's coupon generator (optional but increases recovery rate)
6. Enable the flow and monitor recovery rate and revenue per recipient in Klaviyo analytics

## Estimated Monthly Cost
- Klaviyo: Free up to 500 contacts; ~$20–$45/month for 1,000–5,000 contacts
- No additional tools required
- **Total: $0–$45/month** — typically recovers 5–15% of abandoned carts, paying for itself quickly
